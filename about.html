About me

Learn More about me

My name is Quinten Claes, and I am a student at Artevelde University College in Ghent. From a young age, I’ve always had a passion for computer technologies. Growing up, I was fascinated by how things work behind the scenes—whether it was tinkering with devices, building websites, or exploring how digital systems come together. It was clear to me early on that I wanted to pursue a career in technology.

As I continue my studies, I focus on developing my skills in web development and understanding the broader tech landscape. Beyond just writing code, I’m excited about creating practical, user-centered solutions that make an impact. I believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems, and I’m always eager to learn more, explore new technologies, and challenge myself with every project.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy hanging out with friends and staying active at the gym. I believe a balance of socializing and staying fit is key to maintaining both mental and physical health. I’m excited for what the future holds and look forward to contributing to the tech world in meaningful ways.
